# ThinkReview - Version 2.2.9 Release Notes

**Release Date:** 8 June 2026

---

## 🎯 What's New

### Buy additional review credits without changing your plan 💳

When you hit your daily review limit, ThinkReview can now offer **credit packs** alongside subscription upgrades. Pick a pack, check out in one click, and keep reviewing after your plan’s daily allowance—**each pack stays valid for one year** from purchase.

### See your credit balance in the extension popup 📊

If you have purchased credits, the popup shows your **additional credits** balance and how long they remain valid. A new **Buy credits** button opens the portal so you can top up anytime.

### Clearer upgrade prompts when limits apply ✨

When you reach your daily review limit—or when follow-up chat in the review panel is blocked by the limit—the extension shows a fuller upgrade screen with plan options and credit packs where available. The review loader stays visible until those options finish loading, so you are less likely to see a blank or half-loaded screen.

### Focused guidance when a PR is too large for the free plan 📐

If your pull request exceeds the free-tier size limit, the upgrade prompt now focuses on **plan upgrades** only—credit packs are hidden in that case so the message stays on the right solution for reviewing bigger PRs.

### Simpler “leave a review” prompt ⭐

The in-product review prompt no longer asks for a star rating. Tap **Leave a Review** and you go straight to the right store listing—**Chrome Web Store** or **Firefox Add-ons**, depending on your browser. If you dismissed an older version of the prompt before 7 June 2026, you may see the updated prompt again.

### Refreshed popup messaging 🎨

The extension popup and welcome screen now describe ThinkReview as **agentic code reviews built into every PR page**, with updated titles and subtitles that better match how the product works today.
