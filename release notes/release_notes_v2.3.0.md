# ThinkReview - Version 2.3.0 Release Notes

**Release Date:** 26 June 2026

---

## 🎯 What's New

### ThinkReview Self-Hosted Gateway for Teams organizations 🏢

Teams subscribers can now choose **ThinkReview Self-Hosted Gateway** as an AI provider in extension settings. Code reviews and follow-up chat in the review panel route through your **on-prem gateway** and your own LLM, so review traffic stays inside your network while sign-in, agents, and billing continue to use ThinkReview cloud.

### Configure and test your gateway from the extension popup ⚙️

A new **Gateway Base URL** section appears when you select the self-hosted provider. Enter your gateway address, **test the connection** to confirm it is reachable and ready, then **save** your settings. The extension asks for permission to reach your gateway host only when needed, so you stay in control of access.

### Clear guidance when self-hosted is not available yet 💡

If you are not on a **Teams** plan, or your gateway URL is missing, ThinkReview shows straightforward messages in the popup and review flow—so you know whether to upgrade, enter a URL, or switch back to ThinkReview Cloud instead of hitting a vague failure.

### More reliable portal sign-in after an extension update 🔄

If you have the ThinkReview portal open when the extension updates or reloads, sign-in sync now fails gracefully instead of throwing errors. Refresh the portal page after updating the extension to complete sign-in sync with the new version.
