# ThinkReview - Version 2.0.0 Release Notes

**Release Date:** 12 March 2026

---

## 🎯 What's New

### New Layout Settings 🎨

Take control of how ThinkReview appears in your workflow with a full set of layout options:

- **Docked Sidebar Mode:** The review panel can now be docked to the **left** or **right** edge of the screen, giving you a more integrated experience alongside your merge request or pull request.
- **Configurable Layout Triggers:** Choose how you open the panel—use a **floating button** or a **sidebar tab**, whichever fits your workflow best.
- **Completion Effects:** Get clear visual feedback when a review is ready—a **shake animation** and **best practice bubble** so you know exactly when your review has finished.
- **Layout Settings Widget:** A new widget in the panel header lets you quickly customize your UI preferences without leaving the review.

---

### Copy All Button 📋

- **Copy entire review:** A new **Copy All** button lets you copy the whole review to your clipboard in one click—ideal for sharing with teammates, pasting into comments, or saving to your notes.
