# ThinkReview - Version 2.2.8 Release Notes

**Release Date:** 3 June 2026

---

## 🎯 What's New

### More reliable sign-in for cloud AI reviews 🔐

ThinkReview now keeps you signed in more securely when you use cloud-powered reviews and related features. After you sign in through the ThinkReview portal, the extension remembers your session in a way that works better with the service behind the scenes—so reviews and account features are less likely to fail because of an outdated login state.

### Clear guidance when your session expires ⏱️

If your sign-in is no longer valid, the review panel and extension popup tell you plainly that **your session has expired** and ask you to **sign in again**, instead of showing a vague error. The integrated review panel updates its heading and message so you know exactly what to do next.

### Smoother recovery after you sign out or lose access 🔄

When the service indicates you need to sign in again, ThinkReview clears the old session locally and prompts you to log back in through the portal. Signing out from the extension also removes the stored authorization so the next person using the browser is not left with a half-finished session.

### Consistent experience across reviews, chat, and account actions ✨

Session handling is aligned across starting a code review, follow-up chat in the panel, usage tracking, and syncing your account—so you get the same “please sign in again” experience wherever cloud features need you to be logged in.
