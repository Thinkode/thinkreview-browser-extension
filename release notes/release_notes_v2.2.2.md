# ThinkReview - Version 2.2.2 Release Notes

**Release Date:** 24 April 2026

---

## 🎯 What's New

### Clearer account context with your extension version 📌

When ThinkReview syncs your account or looks up your user profile, the extension now shares its **version number** with our services (read from the extension manifest). That helps us support you faster, roll out improvements responsibly, and keep behavior aligned with the build you are actually running. If the browser APIs needed to read the version are not available, nothing extra is sent—your existing flows keep working as before.
