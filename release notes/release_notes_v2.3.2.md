# ThinkReview - Version 2.3.2 Release Notes

**Release Date:** 5 July 2026

---

## 🎯 What's New

### TR-Gateway badge for self-hosted reviews 🏢

When you use **ThinkReview Self-Hosted Gateway**, the review panel now shows a **TR-Gateway** badge in the metadata bar instead of the **Change Model** link. This makes it clear your review is powered by your on-prem gateway and avoids sending you to cloud model selection when your gateway manages the model.

### Clearer review metadata for Teams organizations ✨

Self-hosted reviews now display a dedicated gateway indicator in the review header, so you can tell at a glance whether a review ran through your organization's gateway or through ThinkReview Cloud.
